If you are receiving this, you are registered for MAGLabs, occurring {{ event_dates() }}, {{ c.EVENT_YEAR }}. This FAQ should answer most of your questions about the event. You may reach us at contact@magfest.org for any questions not covered here.

Will badges be sold at the door?
Yes. Full three-day badges will be available for $60, and one day badges will be available for $25 for Friday, $35 for Saturday, and $15 for Sunday. Children 5 & under are free, and children aged between 6 to 12 receive a half price discount. We do not anticipate selling out, so you should have no problem purchasing a badge at the event.

Where can I park for the weekend?
The Mark Center has a garage with parking for $15/day with a voucher, which you can pick up at Registration. If the Mark Center's garage fills up (which we expect it to), the parking attendants will direct you to the nearby lot at 4900 Seminary Road. Parking there will be around $25 for Friday, and $10 for Saturday or Sunday.

Where do I go when I arrive? How do I pick up my badge?
The first step is to go to the registration desk, located in the Pan Am Foyer near the escalators and elevators near the front desk to pick up your badge. The registration desk is open from 10 AM—2 AM Friday and Saturday, and 10 AM—2 PM Sunday. If you pre-registered, simply show government issued photo ID with date of birth such as a driver's license or military ID, to the registration staffers. On rare occasions we will accept a photo ID without a date of birth such as a high school or college ID. Please note, we do reserve the right to refuse these IDs and anyone using a high school photo ID will also need to bring a signed parental consent form, which must be notarized if they are not accompanied by a legal guardian regardless of age.

Which registration line do I use?

  • Attendees who purchased a standard badge should use the "Preregistration" line.
  • Bands, Guests, Panelists, Staff, and Volunteers should use the line that lists those categories.
  • If you are not already registered for a badge, register at our At-Door Kiosks and then use the "At-Door Registration" line.

Where can I pick up swag that I pre-ordered, such as a t-shirt or supporter bundle?
After you've picked up your badge, you can pick up your swag at the MAGFest merch booth. The merch booth is located near the base of the escalators.

Where can I view a schedule of events?
Use the Guidebook app on a computer or mobile device: Go to the Guidebook website to view the guidebook in your web browser. Make sure to check for Guidebook updates periodically through the weekend, as we may make changes to the schedule.

Can I check if I'm pre-registered?
Use this page to check your registration status.

Do you provide refunds or badge transfers?
We do not provide refunds, but we do allow you to sell or transfer your badge to anyone you wish. E-mail regsupport@magfest.org from the original e-mail address you registered with (or provide the original e-mail) along with your full name, and we will send you a link to transfer your badge as soon as we can. The badge transfer link is also available in the original confirmation email.

Only questions about badge transfers, badge confirmations, or general registration questions should be e-mailed to the registration desk e-mail address. We are unable to respond to any other inquiries.
